Our Rules & Regulations

On admission to any course of study, every student submits himself/herself to disciplinary jurisdiction of the management of the Institute, the Incharge and other officers of the institute.
The students are advised to keep themselves aware of the Institute rules. They are also advised to go through detailed Hostel rules and Prevention of Ragging rules given separately. Ignorance of rules shall not be an excuse for violation.
Each student shall conduct herself/himself, both within and outside the campus of the Institute in a manner befitting a student of a prestigious institute. Each student shall show due respect and courtesy to the teachers.
